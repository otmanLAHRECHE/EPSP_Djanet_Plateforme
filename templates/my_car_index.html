Welcome to MyCAR

MyCAR App description

MyCAR is an ANDROID application for trace the maintenance of users' cars, and its states. It like a digital service book that help the user to trace his car state easily and most importantly, he can get advices of good habits for the safety of the car, and recommendation to warn him of any futuristic maintenance.

MyCAR Features

Easy trace maintenance

The android make adding or tracing history of the car maintenance very easily without any paper work and pens.

Data synchronisation

In order to keep the maintenance history safe from being lost, the app sync the local data with online server and you data will be always available.

Maintenance reminder

In the case of engine oil change forgetting will be big problem for the safety of the car, the app can reminder the user periodically.

buy a car with full service maintenance

when you buy a used car, with MyCAR app you can easily get its service book with it if the seller user have the app.

use app even when off-line

the app save the maintenance data locally and synced automatically, that make it easy for the user.

MyCAR Maintenance Recommendation

MyCAR server can store the history of cars maintenance, than processing this data, and recommend to the user next maintenance to be checked based on the previous failure.

That concept is known as predictive maintenance with Off-Board data.
